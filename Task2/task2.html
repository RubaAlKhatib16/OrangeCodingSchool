Petra

Inhabited since ancient times, this Nabataean caravan-city, situated between the Red Sea and the Dead Sea , was important crossroads between Arabia,Egypu and Syris-phoenicia. Petra is half-built,half carved into the rock, and is surounded by mountains riddled with passages and gorges. It is one of the world's most famous archaeological sites. Where ancient Easterntraditions blend with Hellenisticarchitecture.
